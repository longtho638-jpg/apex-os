# Cold Outreach Templates (DM)

**Goal**: Get 100 replies -> 10 conversions.
**Strategy**: Low friction, high curiosity. Don't sell, just start a conversation.

---

### 1. The "Question" Hook (Best for Twitter/Telegram)
> Hey [Name], quick q - are you tracking your crypto rebates from Binance/OKX manually right now?
>
> Most traders I talk to are losing ~30% monthly because of expired claims.
>
> I built a tool to automate it (ApexOS). Worth a look?

### 2. The "Value First" Hook (Best for Reddit DMs)
> Saw your post about trading fees. Legit pain.
>
> I actually built a dashboard that tracks all those rebates across exchanges automatically.
>
> Happy to give you a free "Founder" account if you want to test it out. No strings attached. Let me know!

### 3. The "Compliment + Problem" Hook
> Hey [Name], been following your trades for a while, solid calls.
>
> Curious - how do you handle rebate reconciliation across exchanges? It's usually a nightmare for high-volume traders.
>
> I built a solution for this. Can I send you a link?

### 4. The "Beta Tester" Invite
> Hi [Name], I'm looking for 10 active traders to beta test my new rebate tracking platform.
>
> It connects to Binance/Bybit via read-only API and finds lost money.
>
> In exchange for feedback, I'll give you a lifetime discount. Interested?

### 5. The "Direct Benefit" Hook
> [Name], if I could show you an extra $500/month in your trading account that you're currently missing, would you be interested?
>
> (It's about automating your rebate claims).

### 6. The "Friction Removal" Hook
> Tracking rebates in spreadsheets is the worst.
>

>
> Want to see a demo?

### 7. The "FOMO" Hook

### 9. The "Short & Punchy"

## 💡 Sending Tips
1. **Personalize**: Always use their name or mention a recent trade.
2. **Profile**: Make sure your bio says "Founder of ApexOS".
3. **Speed**: Reply to responses within 5 minutes.
4. **Platform**:
   - Twitter DMs: Best for networking.
   - Discord: Good for "Value First" hook.
   - Reddit: Be careful, easy to get banned. Use "Value First".

# Growth Strategy: From 0 to 35,000 Users

**Date**: 2025-11-27
**Philosophy**: Product-Led Growth powered by Agentic Content.

## 📅 Monthly Tactics

### Month 1: The Seed (Foundation)
**Goal**: 100 Paid Users ($2k MRR).
**Tactics**:
- **Manual Outreach**: DM 50 crypto traders daily on Twitter/Discord.
- **Waitlist**: Launch "ApexOS Early Access" landing page.
- **Incentive**: "Lifetime Founder Status" for first 100.
**Agents**:
- `SalesAgent`: Scrapes Twitter for "high volume trader" bio keywords.
- `MarketAgent`: Drafts personalized DMs.

### Month 2: The Content Engine (Organic)
**Goal**: 500 Paid Users ($18k MRR).
**Tactics**:
- **SEO Blitz**: Publish 30 articles/month ("Binance vs Bybit Rebates", "AI Trading Bots Review").
- **Twitter Threads**: Daily thread dissecting a crypto narrative.
- **Newsletter**: Weekly "Alpha Report" (AI-generated).
**Agents**:
- `MarketAgent`: Writes & Publishes automatically.
- `DataAgent`: Provides unique data points for content (e.g., "Most traded pair this week").

### Month 3: The Viral Loop (Copy Trading Launch)
**Goal**: 1,500 Paid Users ($70k MRR).
**Tactics**:
- **Leaderboard**: Public ranking of top traders (verified PnL).
- **Shareable PnL**: One-click "Share my 500% gain" image generator.
- **Referral V2**: "Give 1 month PRO, Get 1 month PRO".
**Agents**:
- `ProductAgent`: Optimizes sharing flow.
- `SupportAgent`: Handles influx of new users.

### Month 4: The Affiliate Army (Scale)
**Goal**: 3,500 Paid Users ($175k MRR).
**Tactics**:
- **Influencer Program**: Offer 30% recurring commission to YouTubers.
- **Direct Outreach**: Contact 100 crypto educators.
- **Integration**: Partner with obscure exchanges for exclusive rebate deals.
**Agents**:
- `SalesAgent`: Finds & Emails influencers.
- `OpsAgent`: Automates payout calculations.

### Month 5-6: Enterprise & Institutional (Moat)
**Goal**: 7,800 Paid Users ($740k MRR).
**Tactics**:
- **White Label**: License "ApexOS" to small exchanges or trading groups.
- **API Access**: Sell data streams to funds.
- **Custom Algos**: Build bespoke strategies for whales ($10k setup + monthly).
**Agents**:
- `SalesAgent`: Enterprise lead hunting.
- `DevAgent`: Deploys white-label instances.

## 📊 Metrics to Watch

| Metric | Target (M6) | Warning Sign |
|--------|-------------|--------------|
| **Traffic** | 100k/mo | < 10k/mo |
| **Free->Paid Conv** | 5% | < 2% |
| **Churn** | < 4% | > 7% |
| **Viral K-Factor** | > 1.1 | < 0.8 |
